Wii Balance Board: VR Snowboard

Skiing down the Alps in your heated apartment at 3 am while wearing nothing but a pair of boxer-shorts sounds ridiculous as well as impossible ?
Well ! At least it's not impossible anymore...





About

After finding an old Wii Balance Board in his apartment, an old friend of mine had the idea to implement a Snowboard game using its mechanics.
Since we both never worked with a piece of hardware like that before, we first had to come up with a solution for implementing it in Unity3D
After several attempts we managed to develop our first proof of concept. But we both weren't quite happy with the outcome.
The CharacterController was clunky and motion sickness was a big problem too. We came up with a solution quite quickly and decided that our proof of concept was finished.
Since this is only a prototype rather than an actual game there will be no download.
